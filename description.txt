Note: GoaHud must be enabled by checking "Enable GoaHud" checkbox from Widgets - GoaHud

- Smoothly animated health, armor, ammo numbers and weapon crosshairs
- Customizable per-weapon crosshairs
- Zoom (bind key ui_goahud_zoom 1)
- Q3 style fragged messages
- Improved spectator controls (attack and jump switches player, crouch toggles freecam)
- Custom WeaponRack, Timer and RaceTimer

Experimental addons not enabled by default:
- Grenade timer
- Lag timer/visualizer
